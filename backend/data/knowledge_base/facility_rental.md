# CSU Chico Facility Rental Guide

## Overview

CSU Chico offers a wide range of venue options for events hosted by university departments, student organizations, and external organizations. From intimate meeting rooms to large performing arts venues, our facilities are available year-round subject to availability and campus scheduling priorities.

## Available Venues

### Laxson Auditorium
- **Capacity:** 1,253 seats
- **Description:** CSU Chico's premier performing arts venue, featuring a full stage, professional lighting and sound systems, backstage dressing rooms, and a grand lobby. Ideal for concerts, lectures, theater performances, and large ceremonies.
- **Rental Rate:** $1,200–$4,500 depending on event type, duration, and day of week
- **Equipment Included:** Stage, fixed seating, basic lighting rig, PA system
- **Additional Services:** Professional AV technician required for all events (additional fee); box office services available through the University Box Office at (530) 898-6333
- **Booking Lead Time:** Minimum **30 days** in advance; 60+ days recommended for peak dates

### BMU Auditorium
- **Capacity:** 430 seats
- **Description:** A flexible auditorium in the Bell Memorial Union with tiered seating, a stage, and built-in AV equipment. Well-suited for lectures, screenings, conferences, and mid-sized performances.
- **Equipment Included:** Projector and screen, PA system, podium
- **Booking Lead Time:** Minimum 30 days in advance

### Bell Memorial Union (BMU) Meeting Rooms
- **Description:** Multiple meeting rooms of varying sizes available in the BMU, ranging from small breakout rooms (10–20 capacity) to larger conference rooms (50–100 capacity).
- **Available Rooms:** BMU 203, BMU 209, BMU 302, BMU 320, and others
- **Equipment:** Projectors, whiteboards, conference tables available in select rooms
- **Best For:** Meetings, workshops, training sessions, club activities

### Wildcat Recreation Center (WREC)
- **Description:** Modern recreation facility with gymnasium courts, fitness studios, and outdoor spaces. Available for athletic events, fitness classes, and recreation-focused programming.
- **Spaces Available:** Main gymnasium, multipurpose studios, outdoor courts
- **Contact:** (530) 898-4567 for WREC-specific rental inquiries

### University Farm
- **Description:** CSU Chico's working agricultural facility available for select outdoor events, educational programs, and community partnerships.
- **Best For:** Outdoor events, agricultural education programs, community fairs
- **Special Requirements:** Event plan must accommodate active farm operations

### Other Campus Spaces
- **Kendall Hall Lawn** – Outdoor event space, popular for tabling and small outdoor gatherings
- **BMU Plazas** – Central outdoor gathering areas, available for tabling events and rallies
- **Classroom and lecture hall reservations** – Contact the Registrar or department with jurisdiction over the space

## Reservation Process

### Step 1: Check Availability
- Contact Facilities Scheduling at (530) 898-6458 or facilities@csuchico.edu to check venue availability
- Alternatively, check the 25Live scheduling system online

### Step 2: Submit a Facility Request Form
- Download or complete the **University Facility Request Form** from csuchico.edu/facilities
- Submit at least **30 days before your event** (45+ days for events involving alcohol)
- Forms submitted less than 30 days in advance may be declined

### Step 3: Review & Approval
- Facilities Scheduling reviews your request for conflicts, compliance, and resource requirements
- You will receive a written confirmation or request for additional information within 5–7 business days
- Approval is not guaranteed until a written confirmation is issued

### Step 4: Fulfill Requirements
Complete all required documentation before your event (see Requirements section below)

### Step 5: Confirm & Pay
- Sign the facility use agreement
- Submit payment or departmental account information
- Final confirmation issued upon receipt of all required documents

## Requirements

### Certificate of Insurance (COI)
- **Required for:** All external organizations and most non-university events
- **Coverage:** Minimum $1,000,000 general liability per occurrence
- **Additional Insured:** Certificate must name **"California State University, Chico"** as an additional insured
- Submit COI to: riskmanagement@csuchico.edu or Kendall Hall 220
- Templates and sample COI language available from Risk Management

### Risk Management Contact
- **Phone:** (530) 898-4321
- **Email:** riskmanagement@csuchico.edu
- Risk Management can advise on insurance requirements and assist with COI review

### Catering Requirements
- **Aramark** is the **exclusive catering provider** for all on-campus events
- Outside food and beverage (other than personal consumption) is generally not permitted at catered events
- Order catering through: [campusdining.csuchico.edu/catering](https://campusdining.csuchico.edu/catering)
- Phone: (530) 898-6373
- Catering orders must be placed at least 5 business days in advance

### AV & Technical Support
- Professional AV/technical support is available for all venues
- **Required** for Laxson Auditorium events; recommended for BMU Auditorium
- AV support involves additional fees billed at hourly or flat-event rates
- Contact Facilities Scheduling to add AV support to your reservation

## Alcohol at Events

Serving alcohol at on-campus events requires special approval and must comply with CSU and California ABC regulations.

### Requirements for Alcohol Permit
1. Complete the **Special Event Alcohol Request Form** (available at csuchico.edu/facilities)
2. Submit at least **45 days before the event**
3. Provide event details including: expected attendance, security plan, and alcohol service plan
4. Obtain approval from University Risk Management and the appropriate vice president
5. A licensed and insured bartender/server is required for all events serving alcohol
6. Aramark must provide all alcohol service (exclusive caterer policy applies)
7. Wristbanding of guests 21+ is required; ID verification mandatory

### Prohibited Situations
- Alcohol may not be served at events where minors are the primary audience
- Alcohol cannot be advertised as a feature of student organization events
- Alcohol is never permitted in classrooms or academic buildings without special written approval

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I reserve a space for a recurring weekly meeting?**
Yes. Recurring reservations are available on a semester basis. Contact Facilities Scheduling to arrange. These must be confirmed each semester.

**What happens if I need to cancel my reservation?**
Cancellations must be made in writing to facilities@csuchico.edu. Cancellation fees may apply depending on how close to the event the cancellation occurs. Review the facility use agreement for specific terms.

**Can external organizations use campus facilities for private events?**
Yes, subject to availability and approval. External organizations are charged 150% of published rates and must provide a Certificate of Insurance. Priority is given to university-affiliated events.

**Is parking included with facility rental?**
No. Parking is managed separately by Parking Services. Event organizers may arrange for visitor parking validation or event-specific permits by contacting Parking Services at (530) 898-5475.

**Are university facilities available during winter and summer breaks?**
Limited availability during break periods. Some facilities close or have reduced staffing. Contact Facilities Scheduling well in advance for break-period events.
