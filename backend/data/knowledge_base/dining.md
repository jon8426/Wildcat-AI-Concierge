# CSU Chico Dining Services

## Overview

CSU Chico offers a variety of on-campus dining options to suit different schedules, dietary needs, and budgets. From the main dining hall to grab-and-go locations and coffee shops, there's something for everyone on campus.

## Campus Dining Locations

### 1. Marketplace — Main Dining Hall
- **Location:** Bell Memorial Union (BMU), Basement Level
- **Hours:**
  - Monday–Friday: 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM
  - Saturday–Sunday: 9:00 AM – 8:00 PM
- **About:** The Marketplace is CSU Chico's primary all-you-care-to-eat dining hall. It features multiple live-action stations including:
  - **Breakfast bar** – eggs, bacon, pancakes, and made-to-order omelets
  - **Grill station** – burgers, grilled chicken, and daily specials
  - **International station** – rotating global cuisine
  - **Pizza & pasta** – fresh-baked pizza and house-made pasta dishes
  - **Salad bar** – fresh vegetables, proteins, and housemade dressings
  - **Deli** – build-your-own sandwiches and wraps
  - **Dessert station** – rotating desserts and soft-serve ice cream
- Accepts all meal plans and Declining Balance (Cat Cash)
- Dining hall swipes accepted; guests may enter with a guest swipe from a meal plan holder

## Meal Plans

Meal plans are available to all enrolled students and are **required** for residents living in university housing. Plans are loaded to your Wildcat ID card.

### Available Meal Plan Options

| Plan | Description | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Unlimited** | Unlimited swipes at Marketplace and Sutter Dining | Residents who eat on campus frequently |
| **Block 175** | 175 meal swipes per semester + $100 Declining Balance | Residents or commuters with moderate appetite |
| **Block 120** | 120 meal swipes per semester + $75 Declining Balance | Commuters or students with lighter dining needs |
| **Declining Balance** | Pre-loaded Cat Cash account, no swipes | Commuters, graduate students, or supplemental use |

- Meal plans are purchased through the Housing & Dining portal at [csuchico.edu/housing](https://csuchico.edu/housing)
- Unused swipes at the end of the semester do **not** roll over (except Declining Balance funds, which carry over within the academic year)
- Meal plan changes can be made during the first two weeks of each semester

### Cat Cash (Declining Balance)
- Accepted at all on-campus dining locations
- Can be added at any time through the online portal or at the Dining Services office
- Purchases made with Cat Cash are **tax-free**
- Also accepted at the Bookstore and select vending machines

## Dietary Accommodations

CSU Chico Dining Services is committed to providing inclusive dining options for all students.

### Available Accommodations
- **Allergen menus:** Available at all locations; posted online and at each station
- **Top 9 allergens** labeled at all Marketplace stations: dairy, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, soy, sesame
- **Vegan and vegetarian** options available at every meal
- **Gluten-sensitive** options available (not a gluten-free kitchen; cross-contamination possible)
- **Halal and Kosher** options available at the Marketplace — check the daily menu
- **Religious or medical dietary needs:** Contact Dining Services at least **48 hours in advance** to arrange accommodations

### How to Request Special Accommodations
1. Contact Dining Services at dining@csuchico.edu or (530) 898-6373
2. Provide at least 48 hours' notice
3. For ongoing medical dietary needs, connect with the Accessibility Resource Center (ARC) at (530) 898-5959

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I use my meal plan off campus?**
No. Meal plan swipes are only valid at on-campus CSU Chico Dining locations. Cat Cash Declining Balance is on-campus only as well.

**What if I have a severe food allergy?**
Contact Dining Services directly at dining@csuchico.edu to arrange a personalized dining plan. Staff can prepare allergen-free meals in a separate area upon request with advance notice.

**Are dining locations open during finals week?**
Hours may vary during finals, holidays, and semester breaks. Check csuchico.edu/dining for updated seasonal hours.

**Can I get a meal plan if I live off campus?**
Yes. Commuter meal plans (Block 175, Block 120, and Declining Balance) are available to all enrolled students.
