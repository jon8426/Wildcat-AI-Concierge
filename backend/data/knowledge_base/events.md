# Events & Campus Activities at CSU Chico

## Overview

CSU Chico has a vibrant campus life with hundreds of events happening every semester — from concerts and lectures to club meetings, athletic events, and cultural celebrations. Whether you're looking to get involved, be entertained, or learn something new, there's always something happening on campus.

---

## Events Calendar

### Wildcat Events Calendar
- **Website:** [events.csuchico.edu](https://events.csuchico.edu)
- **Platform:** Localist (community events platform)

## Associated Students (AS)

Associated Students is the student government body at CSU Chico and one of the primary sponsors of campus programming and events.

- **Location:** Bell Memorial Union (BMU), Room 220
- **Phone:** (530) 898-6281
- **Website:** csuchico.edu/as
- AS sponsors weekly events including concerts, movie nights, cultural showcases, wellness activities, and social mixers
- AS allocates funding to recognized student organizations for event programming
- Student government meetings are open to all students
- AS elections held each spring semester

- **Location:** Laxson Auditorium (lobby)
- **Phone:** (530) 898-6333

## Common Event Areas & Gathering Spaces

### Indoor Spaces
- **BMU Auditorium** – 430-seat venue for lectures, screenings, and performances
- **Laxson Auditorium** – 1,253-seat performing arts venue
- **BMU Meeting Rooms** – Various sizes for club meetings, workshops, and small gatherings
- **Meriam Library Group Study Rooms** – Bookable for small groups

### Outdoor Spaces
- **BMU Plazas (North & South)** – Central outdoor gathering areas; popular for tabling, music, and informal events
- **Kendall Hall Lawn** – Open grassy area used for outdoor events, rallies, and demonstrations
- **Free Speech Area** – Designated space for amplified speech and public expression on campus

### Off-Campus Adjacent
- **Bidwell Park** – World-class city park directly adjacent to the east side of campus; used for recreation, nature walks, and community events. One of the largest municipal parks in the US.
- **Downtown Chico** – Vibrant downtown district approximately 0.5 miles from campus with restaurants, shops, music venues, and the Saturday Certified Farmers Market

## Recognized Student Organizations

CSU Chico has over **200 recognized student organizations** across a wide range of interests.

- **Directory:** [csuchico.edu/orgs](https://csuchico.edu/orgs)
- Categories include: Academic & Professional, Cultural & International, Greek Life, Honors, Political, Religious & Spiritual, Service & Advocacy, Sports & Recreation, and Special Interest
- **Starting a new organization:** Contact the Associated Students office (BMU 220) for the recognition process
- New organizations must have at least 10 student members and a faculty/staff advisor
- Recognized organizations receive access to free room reservations, AS funding opportunities, and tabling space

### How to Get Involved
1. Browse the organization directory at csuchico.edu/orgs
2. Attend the Activities Fair at the start of each semester (BMU Plazas)
3. Contact the organization's listed email or attend their open meetings
4. Check the Wildcat Events Calendar for club info sessions

## Reserving Space for Events

### 25Live — Campus Scheduling System
- Student organizations and university departments reserve spaces using **25Live**
- Access at: 25live.csuchico.edu (log in with CSU Chico credentials)
- Search available rooms, view room capacities, and submit reservation requests
- Reservations typically confirmed within 3–5 business days
- For large events (300+ attendees), contact University Facilities Scheduling directly at (530) 898-6458

### Event Setup & Support
- Tables, chairs, and basic AV equipment available for reserved spaces
- Request setup needs when submitting your 25Live reservation
- Custodial and security staff may be required and billed for large events

## Guest Speaker Policy

Bringing an outside speaker to campus? Here's what you need to know:

- Contact **Student Affairs** (Kendall Hall 110) at least **3 weeks in advance**
- Required for events where a speaker:
  - Receives an honorarium or travel reimbursement
  - Is traveling specifically for the event
  - Represents a controversial or sensitive topic requiring security planning
- Student Affairs will assess security, venue, and logistical needs
- The university is committed to free inquiry and invites speakers across the ideological spectrum
- Contact: (530) 898-6451

## Farmers Market

While not on campus, the downtown Chico Farmers Market is a popular weekly destination for students.

- **Day:** Wednesdays
- **Hours:** 7:30 AM – 1:00 PM
- **Location:** Downtown Chico (2nd and Wall Streets), approximately 2 miles from campus
- Fresh produce, local vendors, food stalls, and live music
- Accessible by bike, car, or city bus (BCAG Transit Route 3 stops near campus and downtown)

## Annual Campus Traditions & Events

- **WildcatWelcome Week** – Beginning of fall semester, orientation events, tabling fairs, concerts
