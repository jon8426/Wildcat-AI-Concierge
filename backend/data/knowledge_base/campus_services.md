# CSU Chico Campus Services

## Overview

CSU Chico offers a comprehensive range of student and campus services to support your academic success, health, and well-being. Below is a guide to the most commonly used services on campus.

---

## Health & Wellness

### Student Health Center
- **Location:** Kendall Hall
- **Phone:** (530) 898-6452
- **Hours:** Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
- **Services:**
  - Primary care and urgent care visits
  - Immunizations and vaccinations
  - Sexual health services and STI testing
  - Lab services and X-ray
  - Physical therapy and sports medicine
  - Pharmacy (prescriptions filled on-site)
  - Travel medicine consultations
- Health Center visits are included in the Student Health Fee; some services have additional costs
- Appointments recommended; walk-ins accepted for urgent needs
- After-hours advice line available by phone

### Counseling Services
- **Location:** Kendall Hall 220
- **Phone:** (530) 898-6345
- **Services:**
  - Individual counseling (short-term)
  - Group therapy and support groups
  - Crisis intervention (same-day appointments available)
  - Psychiatric services and medication management
  - Workshops on stress, anxiety, relationships, and more
  - Referral services for long-term therapy needs
- All services are confidential and included in the Student Health Fee
- Students experiencing a mental health emergency should call (530) 898-6345 during business hours or 911 after hours
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text HOME to 741741

## Academic & Enrollment Services

### Financial Aid Office
- **Location:** Student Services Center (SSC), Room 270
- **Phone:** (530) 898-6451
- **Services:**
  - FAFSA assistance and financial aid advising
  - Grants, scholarships, loans, and work-study
  - Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP) appeals
  - Emergency financial assistance funds
  - Financial literacy workshops
- **FAFSA Priority Deadline:** March 2 for California residents (Cal Grant consideration)
- Visit csuchico.edu/financialaid for forms and resources

### Registrar's Office
- **Location:** Student Services Center (SSC), Room 140
- **Phone:** (530) 898-6452
- **Services:**
  - Class registration and enrollment
  - Transcript requests (official and unofficial)
  - Enrollment and degree verifications
  - Diploma and graduation services
  - Grade changes and record corrections
  - FERPA consent and records release forms
- Transcripts ordered online at csuchico.edu/registrar
- Official transcripts: $10 per copy (electronic or paper)

### Office of Admissions
- **Location:** Kendall Hall
- **Phone:** (530) 898-4428
- **Services:**
  - Undergraduate and graduate application assistance
  - Transfer student advising
  - Campus tours and information sessions
  - Application status inquiries
  - International student admission support
- Apply online at: [csumentor.edu](https://csumentor.edu) or [csuchico.edu/admissions](https://csuchico.edu/admissions)

- **Services:**

  - Student organization advising and recognition
  - Dean of Students services
  - Basic needs support (food, housing insecurity)
  - Advocacy and support for students in crisis

### University Housing
- **Main Office:** Lassen Hall
- **Phone:** (530) 898-6204
- **Email:** housing@csuchico.edu
- **Website:** csuchico.edu/housing
- **Services:**
  - Residence hall applications and assignments
  - Meal plan enrollment and changes
  - Room change requests
  - Residential programming and community events

## Safety & Emergency Services

- **Emergency:** (530) 898-5555 *(or dial 9-1-1)*
- **Non-Emergency:** (530) 898-5372
- **Location:** Sacramento Avenue (south end of campus)
- **Services:**
  - 24/7 emergency response
  - Campus safety escorts (WalkSafe program)
  - Lost and found
  - Parking citation appeals
  - Crime prevention programs
  - Clery Act crime statistics and campus safety reports

- Sign up for **WildCat Alert** emergency notifications at csuchico.edu/wildcatalert
- Text and email alerts for campus emergencies, weather, and safety incidents
