# CSU Chico Campus Map & Navigation Guide

## Overview

CSU Chico's main campus spans approximately 119 acres in Chico, California, bordered by Bidwell Park to the east. The campus is designed for walkability, with most buildings within a 10–15 minute walk of each other. This guide will help you find your way around.

## Key Landmarks & Buildings

### Administrative & Student Services

**Kendall Hall** *(Administration Hub)*
- Central administrative building and one of the most identifiable campus landmarks
- Houses: President's Office, Admissions, Financial Aid (nearby), Student Health Center, Counseling Services, Student Affairs, Parking Services (Room 130), Facilities Scheduling (Room 220)
- Located in the heart of campus near the main entrance

**Student Services Center (SSC)**
- Houses: Accessibility Resource Center (Room 170), Financial Aid (Room 270), Registrar (Room 140)
- One-stop shop for enrollment and student support services

### Student Life

**Bell Memorial Union (BMU)** *(Student Hub)*
- The center of student life on campus
- Houses: Marketplace dining hall (basement), Wildcat Den (main floor), Bookstore (Room 130), Associated Students (Room 220), BMU Auditorium, multiple meeting rooms, lounges, and study spaces
- BMU Plazas (North and South) are popular outdoor gathering spots flanking the building
- Central location — most students pass through the BMU area daily

### Academic Buildings

**Meriam Library** *(Campus Center Landmark)*
- The most central and recognizable building on campus
- Serves as the main navigation landmark — most campus directions reference the Library
- Houses: Main library collections, reference desk, IT Help Desk (Room 142), group study rooms, Meriam Library Café
- Elevator access to all floors
- Wraps & More café on the ground floor

**Butte Hall**
- Major academic building housing multiple departments
- Features a distinctive architectural design
- Elevator access available

**Langdon Hall**
- Home to social sciences and humanities departments
- Elevator access available

**Tehama Hall**
- Academic building in the central campus area
- Houses several College of Humanities and Fine Arts departments

**PLMS — Physical Learning & Media Services Building**
- Houses: Starbucks (Room 102), media production facilities, language labs, and instructional technology resources
- Located in the eastern portion of campus

**Holt Hall**
- College of Business building, east side of campus

**Yolo Hall**
- Science and engineering classrooms and labs

### Performing Arts

**Laxson Auditorium**
- CSU Chico's premier performing arts venue (1,253 seats)
- Home to Theatre CSU Chico, University Symphony Orchestra, and Chico Performances
- Box office in the lobby, professional stage and backstage facilities
- Located on the western side of campus along W 2nd Street

### Recreation & Athletics

**Wildcat Recreation Center (WREC)** *(North Campus)*
- State-of-the-art student recreation facility
- Houses: fitness center, gymnasium, rock climbing wall, racquetball courts, group fitness studios, aquatics (Glenn Hall Pool nearby)
- Located at the north end of campus near the residence halls

**Acker Gym**
- Home of Wildcat basketball and volleyball
- Located near the WREC

## Transportation & Getting Around

### Campus Shuttle
- **Free** for all enrolled students with a valid Wildcat ID
- Routes cover main campus locations and residence halls
- **Routes and schedules:** [csuchico.edu/transportation](https://csuchico.edu/transportation)
- Shuttle stops marked with blue signage throughout campus
- Limited service on evenings and weekends; check posted schedules

### Bicycle Parking
- Bike parking racks located throughout campus near most buildings
- **Covered racks** available at:
  - Bell Memorial Union (BMU)
  - Meriam Library
- Additional racks at: Kendall Hall, WREC, Student Services Center, and residence halls
- Secure bike lockers available — contact Parking Services for locker rental
- Bicycle registration recommended; contact UOPD for free registration

### Walking
- Most campus buildings are within a 10–15 minute walk
- Walking paths are well-lit; campus lighting maps available at csuchico.edu/facilities

### Driving & Parking
- See the full **Parking Guide** for permit types, lot locations, and pay station information
- Major parking areas: Lots 7, 8, 10 (visitor), 22, 23 (faculty/staff), 11, 27 (EV charging), and peripheral student lots
- Rideshare (Uber/Lyft) drop-off/pick-up: BMU front entrance and Kendall Hall areas

### City Bus (BCAG Transit)
- Butte County transit serves the campus area
- Routes connect campus to downtown Chico and other neighborhoods
- Free with student ID on select routes — check csuchico.edu/transportation for details
- Main bus stop: Near Chestnut Street entrance

## Accessibility Navigation

### Accessible Routes
- All primary pathways on campus are ADA accessible
- Detailed accessible route maps: [csuchico.edu/facilities/accessibility-maps](https://csuchico.edu/facilities/accessibility-maps)
- Routes show curb cuts, ramps, accessible building entrances, and rest areas

### Elevator Locations
- **Meriam Library** – Central elevator, all floors accessible
- **Bell Memorial Union (BMU)** – Elevator serving basement through upper floors
- **Butte Hall** – Main elevator
- **Langdon Hall** – Elevator access
- **Kendall Hall** – Elevator to all floors
- Additional elevators available in most multi-story academic buildings

### ADA Parking
- Blue Zone spaces in every campus lot, closest to building entrances
- Valid state ADA placard or CSU Chico Blue permit required

### Campus Escort Service
- Available for students and visitors with mobility needs
- Contact ARC at (530) 898-5959 to arrange
- UOPD WalkSafe program also available for evening safety escorts: (530) 898-5372
